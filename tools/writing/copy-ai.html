Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Copy.ai

What makes Copy.ai different from other AI writing tools?

Copy.ai has evolved beyond simple AI writing into a comprehensive GTM (Go-To-Market) platform. It offers advanced workflows, data intelligence, and automation capabilities that connect multiple business processes, making it more than just a content generation tool.

Can I use Copy.ai for free?

Yes, Copy.ai offers a generous free plan with 2,000 words per month, access to 90+ copywriting tools, and 7 languages. This is perfect for testing the platform and small-scale content needs.

What are Copy.ai workflows and how do they work?

Workflows are automated sequences that connect multiple AI actions, data sources, and integrations. You can create complex automation chains that handle everything from lead generation to content creation and distribution, all triggered by specific events or schedules.

Does Copy.ai integrate with other business tools?

Yes, Copy.ai offers 50+ native integrations with popular tools like HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, and Google Workspace. It also supports Zapier connections and custom API integrations for enterprise users.

How does Copy.ai's brand voice feature work?

Brand Voice allows you to train Copy.ai's AI to write in your specific tone and style. You can upload sample content, define voice characteristics, and create multiple brand voice profiles for different use cases or clients.

Is Copy.ai suitable for team collaboration?

Absolutely. Copy.ai offers team workspaces, shared templates, approval workflows, role-based permissions, and version control. Teams can collaborate seamlessly while maintaining brand consistency and quality control.

What languages does Copy.ai support?

Copy.ai supports 25+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, and many more. The free plan includes 7 languages, while paid plans unlock all supported languages.

Can I cancel my Copy.ai subscription anytime?

Yes, Copy.ai subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You'll continue to have access to paid features until the end of your current billing period.
